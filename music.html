Discography


Music has given me freedom to not only express myself, but also be a part of the creative process.

Passion Play

Passion Play combines New Jack Swing with Funk and Electronica to deliver a concept album that is unlike any other. This album is a paradigm shift in my musical sound as it incorporates grandiloquent harmonies and lush synths. It is heavily inspired by artists like Timbaland, Teddy Riley and The Neptunes.

Momentum

Momentum is an album stripped down to its bare essence, with minimal production entirely focused on Dance Music and simplicity. It is inspired by electronic acts such as Pendulum and Major Lazer, and artists such as Diplo, DJ Snake and Kygo.

Kingdom Come

Kingdom Come is an instrumental album that is a massive celebration of European and Asian Culture, by combining Electronic Music and Alternative Pop with World Music and island style percussion. The album is inspired by artists such as William Orbit, Devi Sri Prasad and Mani Sharma.

Nightfade

Nightfade is an instrumental synthwave album that combines retro sounding instruments such as synthesizers such as the Moog and the CS-80, and drum machines such as the Linn Drum with modern production techniques. It is inspired by Daft Punk, John Carpenter and Noah '40' Shebib.

The Struggle++

The Struggle++ comprises of tracks that I had made during my initial days of music production. It is a highly experimental album that combines Hip-Hop, Lo-Fi and Industrial Music. It is inspired by Nine Inch Nails, Alex Da Kid and Kanye West.
